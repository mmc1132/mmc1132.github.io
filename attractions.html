Attractions

Things to do

Explore Taniti

Taniti is full of unique destinations to explore! We are most well known for our beaches, rainforests, and volcano. Wanting to optimize relaxation and exposure to the sun? Miles of sandy beaches will accomadate you and your party well. Prefer to adventure? Head to any of our rainforests to hike, zip-line, and experience the thriving ecosystem. Looking for a more secluded destination? Taniti is also home to a number of rocky beaches only accessible by boat which may be desirable depending on your accomadations!

Water Activities

The island is a haven for water activities! Many visitors take advantage of the beautiful coral reefs, plentiful with fish, by snorkeling! It is also a very popular fishing destination. Therefore, there are many chartered fishing tours that can be taken across the island!

Sightseeing

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Other popular activities include boat or bus tours of the island, hikes in the rainforest, or visits to Taniti's active volcano.

Nightlife

When visitors need to escape the sun and elements, they can take advantage of the islands nightlife! Whether you are looking to enjoy a drink at one of our pubs, such as the microbrewery, or bust a move at the new club, the island should have exactly what you are looking for to end your night in the best way.

Additional Activities

Taniti also offers other activities such as movies, helicopter tours, art galleries, a museum, an arcade, bowling, and as of next year: a nine-hole golf course!
