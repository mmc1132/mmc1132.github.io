Welcome!

Welcome and thank you for considering your vacation here on Taniti, a small tropical island in the Pacific! Varied terrain including both sandy and rocky beaches, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior where a small active volcano resides are awaiting exploration! Whether it be a wedding, family vacation, honeymoon, or even business events, this can be the perfect destination no matter the occasion! On our site you can find more information regarding local attractions, lodging, food, and much more!
