Getting To the Island

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. We are also in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years!

Getting Around the Island

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport. Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law). Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.

Ground Transportation Companies

  • Taniti Taxi - A local taxi service that operates throughout the island.
  • Taniti Rentals - Offers car and bike rentals for exploring the island at your own pace.
  • Taniti Bus Service - Provides public bus services connecting major areas of the island.
  • Hertz Rental - Provides additonal car rentals to get around!
