Lodging

Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging accomedations that ranges from inexpensive hostels to a large, four-star resort; this range of options are sure to meet every individuals specific needs and finances! Additionally, the island has many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts.

A Safe Stay For You

Our promise to you is to ensure the best lodging experience possible! We promise to ensure that all of our lodging is tightly regulated and inspected by appropriate agencies to help keep you safe and clean so you can best experience your stay on the island!
